## Hi there 👋, I'm Mingzhe Li!

- 🔭  I am a Ph.D. student jointly at the College of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence, Fudan University, and the Shanghai Innovation Institute, advised by [Prof. Xipeng Qiu](https://xpqiu.github.io/).
